* **The Problem:** Emerging markets lack the infrastructure for traditional credit scoring.

* **Industry Impact:** Venture Capital and Neobanking.

### 4. Smart City Infrastructure Currency
* **The Problem:** Cities struggle to manage micro-transactions for energy and transit.
* **The BETH Solution:** A stabilized internal currency designed for high-frequency, low-fee tech environments.
* **Industry Impact:** Government Tech (GovTech) and Renewable Energy.
